# Product Guidelines

## Visual Identity
**Style:** Tech-Centric & Modern
- **Aesthetic:** Bold, high-contrast design with a focus on dark mode elements that highlight technical expertise.
- **Imagery:** High-quality images of internal components, tools, and successful repairs to showcase capability.
- **Color Palette:** A technical palette (e.g., deep blues, slate greys, electric accents) that conveys precision and modernity.
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif fonts that are highly legible on screens, reinforcing a clean, digital-first look.

## Tone of Voice
**Tone:** Professional & Expert
- **Key Attributes:** Authoritative, precise, reliable, and reassuring.
- **Approach:** Communicate deep technical knowledge without overwhelming the user. Emphasize certifications, specialized tools, and the quality of parts used.
- **Messaging:** "We know your device inside and out." Focus on competence and the guarantee of a perfect repair.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Mobile-First Design:** Given the nature of the service (broken devices), the experience must be flawless on mobile devices.
- **Radical Transparency:** Clear display of estimated costs and repair times to establish immediate trust.
- **Frictionless Conversion:** Reduce barriers to entry. Essential actions like "Get a Quote" or "Contact via WhatsApp" must be accessible from anywhere on the site.
- **Performance:** Speed is a feature. The site must load instantly to prevent bounce rates from frustrated users with potentially damaged devices.
